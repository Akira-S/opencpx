***CONFIDENTIAL - FOR INTERNAL DISTRIBUTION ONLY*** 

TO: All Verio Associates 
DATE: January 15, 2005
SUBJECT: Organizational Announcement

Dear Verio Colleagues, 

It is with regret that I announce that Jimmy Treuting has decided to resign
from Verio. His last day will be June 30, 2005. We would like to thank Jimmy
for his contributions and commitment to Verio over the years.  

Jimmy joined Verio as part of the acquisition of Communique, Inc., in
October 1997, where he was founder, Chairman and CEO. As the head of
Communique, Jimmy built the company into one of the leading ISP's in the
Southeast and one of the few profitable and growing ISP's in the country. 

After heading the integration and growth efforts for Verio's southeastern
U.S. regional operations, Verio tapped into Jimmy's experience in sales and
sales management by placing him in the role of Vice President of Sales,
where he helped form the strategy and structure of our national sales
organization. Jimmy then served as Verio's President of Access/Broadband and
as President of eBusiness Solutions from September 2001 until March 2002,
when he left the company. 

In June 2003, Jimmy rejoined Verio as the Acting President of Broadband. He
later transitioned to the role of Acting President of Network Services, and
also took on a special assignment as the leader of North America Sales,
where he participated in a task force effort to consolidate sales for the
company. Most recently, Jimmy has served as Special Advisor to assist with
the transition of the Sales organization into the newly organized
market-focused operations team led by Calvin Quan. 

As you can see, Jimmy leaves a legacy of significant and diverse
contributions to Verio's sales organization and customer operations. Please
join us in extending our sincerest appreciation to Jimmy and in wishing him
great success in his future endeavors. 

 

***CONFIDENTIAL - FOR INTERNAL DISTRIBUTION ONLY***
